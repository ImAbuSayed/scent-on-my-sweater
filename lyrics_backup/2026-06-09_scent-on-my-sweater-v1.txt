[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
There's a lingering whisper from the night we met
A soft kind of magic I could never forget
I'm holding onto moments when we were together
Still breathing in the scent on my sweater

[Verse 1]
The porch light is humming a flickering tune
We were tangled up under a pale silver moon
You rested your head right against my chest
In that quiet evening, I found my rest

[Pre-Chorus]
Every time the wind blows across the plain
I can feel the warmth rushing through my veins
A slow-burning fire that refuses to fade
I'm lost in the beautiful mess that we made

[Chorus]
Oh, the scent on my sweater is pulling me close
It's the ghost of your touch that I want the most
A sweet summer trace in the cool autumn air
Whenever I close my eyes, I swear you're right there

[Post-Chorus]
Just the scent on my sweater
I could hold onto this feeling forever

[Verse 2]

Reliving the echoes of a night that has passed
